Questions

Good questions, answered.

Who qualifies as a KREDS member? +
Anyone currently working in Copenhagen hospitality — bartenders, chefs, sommeliers, servers, floor managers, hotel staff, baristas. We verify employment at sign-up through a quick document check.
How does verification work? +
Upload proof of current hospitality employment — a recent payslip, contract, or uniform badge photo. Verification typically completes within 24 hours. Your digital member card activates immediately after approval.
When does KREDS launch? +
Public launch is 1 June 2026. Waitlist members get early access the week before, plus a guaranteed founding rate if they're in the first 100 confirmations.
What happens when my 3-month trial ends? +
You'll get a reminder a week before your trial ends. You can pick any plan — monthly, annual, or founding — or cancel with one tap. No auto-charges without a clear heads-up.
Does it cost my venue anything to join? +
No. Listing on KREDS is free — no signup fee, no monthly subscription, no commission on member visits. Venues set their own discount, their own days, and can pause any time.
What counts as a "genuine discount"? +
Venues set their own terms, but we require at least a meaningful offer — typically 20–50% off food, drink, or full bills on specified days. No "10% on filter coffee" filler. If a listing doesn't meet the bar, it doesn't go up.
Can I cancel? +
Yes, any time, from inside the app — no email required, no retention calls. Monthly cancels immediately. Annual runs to the end of the paid period.
